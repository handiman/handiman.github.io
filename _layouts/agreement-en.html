Term and termination

This Agreement shall commence on {{ page.scope.start_date}} and shall remain in force for a period of {{ page.scope.period }}. Unless the Agreement has been terminated by the Client, in writing, {{ page.termination.notice_period }} prior to the end of the said {{ page.scope.period }} period, the Agreement shall automatically be renewed for successive period of one {{ page.scope.period }}, with a one {{ page.termination.notice_period }} written notice period (the “Term”).

This Agreement replaces any previous agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

Each party has the right to terminate this Agreement with immediate effect if the other party fails to fulfill, or breaches, its obligations under the Agreement and if capable of remedy, the breach is not remedied within {{ page.termination.notice_period }} after written notice from the non-breaching Party.

The Client is also entitled to terminate the Agreement with immediate effect and claim damages from the Consultant if the Consultant:

  1. is at fault and this affects {{ client.name }};
  2. is suspected of any crime of relevance for the provided Services;
  3. is negligent or incompetent in the performance of the Services; or
  4. is incapable of performing the Services, in whole or in part, in such a manner as is reasonably intended or specified in the Agreement.

The termination or expiry of the Agreement, irrespective of the reason thereto, shall not affect the validation of any provision in this Agreement that explicitly, or implicitly, is intended to continue to apply after the expiry, or termination, of the Agreement.

Insurance

The Consultant shall at its own expense, obtain and maintain adequate insurance coverage for the Services, which shall include liability insurance. The insurance shall cover the both the Consultant and the Services and be valid for the Term. The Consultant shall, if requested by the Client, provide proof of the adequate insurance coverage.

Legal position of the Consultant

The Consultant and employees of the Consultant that carry out the Services are independent in relation to the Client, and no employment relation shall arise between such employees of the Consultant, and the Client, as a result of this Agreement. The Consultant may not represent or bind the Client in relation to any third party.
